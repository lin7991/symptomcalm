About SymptomCalm

What We Do

SymptomCalm is an educational resource that explores common health symptoms through the lens of Traditional Chinese Medicine (TCM). Our goal is to make TCM's perspective on health accessible, grounded, and practical — without mysticism, without miracle claims, and without jargon.

Who This Is For

This site is for anyone who:

  • Has a chronic symptom that conventional medicine hasn't fully addressed
  • Is curious about TCM but doesn't know where to start
  • Wants to understand their body through a different framework
  • Is looking for complementary approaches alongside their existing healthcare

Our Approach

We believe that TCM has something valuable to offer modern health conversations — not as a replacement for Western medicine, but as a complementary perspective. We present TCM concepts using analogies and language that make sense to a modern audience, while staying true to the traditional framework.

Our Commitment

  • Accuracy: We base our content on established TCM theory and cite modern research where relevant.
  • Transparency: We clearly distinguish between traditional theories and modern scientific evidence.
  • Safety: Every article includes a medical disclaimer and encourages readers to consult qualified healthcare providers.
  • No miracle claims: We present TCM as a complementary approach, not a miracle cure.
